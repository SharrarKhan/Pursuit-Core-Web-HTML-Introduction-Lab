The Perfect PB&J

Description

This is one of my favourite sandwiches

Ingredients

  1. 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  2. 1 jar of jelly
  3. 1 jar of penuter butter
  4. 2 sharp knife
  5. A cutting board

Directions

  1. Put two slices of the bread on the cutting board
  2. Use one knife to scoop up the penut butter and the other to scoop up the jelly
  3. Then spread them over on on the bread. Spread the PB on one slice and the jelly on the other
  4. Put the sandwiches together and enjoy
